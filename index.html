Welcome to Seaside Hotel & Resort

Seaside Hotel and Resort is a hotel and resort destination that offers beautiful views of Jimbaran beach. In addition to being presented with a very beautiful view of the beach, Seaside Hotel and Resort can be easily found, just 10 kilometers from Bali Ngurah Rai International Airport. Seaside Hotel provides convenience and comfort for you, by providing excellent access to all dining places, hotel facilities, and recreation areas that have been provided. Free shuttle facilities around the resort area can be enjoyed exclusively at Seaside Resort. Includes easy access to the world-class Spa, bar, restaurant, swimming pool, golf course, fitness center and free Wi-Fi access throughout the Seaside Resort. Seaside Hotel and Resort is also equipped with wedding facilities, meetings, and honeymoon packages that will certainly pamper guests with the comfort of the Resort and beautiful views of Jimbaran beach.
